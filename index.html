Chief Obafemi Awolowo

First Premier of Western Nigeria

Here is the timeline of Chief Awolowo's life:

  • 1909 - Born to a Yoruba farmer in Ikenne, Western Region, British Nigeria.
  • 1927 - He enrolled at University of London and graduated with degree of Bachelor of Commerce (Hons).
  • 1944 - He studied law at University of London and was later called to bar.
  • 1949 - He founded the Nigerian Tribune, a private Nigerian newspaper.
  • 1954 - He became the first premier of the Western Region.
  • 1959 - He established the first television service in Africa and Oduduwa group mainly financed from lucrative cocoa industry.
  • 1967 - He was appointed as the federal commisioner for finance.
  • 1987 - He died in his sleep on his bed in his hometown, Ikenne.
